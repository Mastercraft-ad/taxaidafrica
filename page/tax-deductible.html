Tax Deductible Donations

Learn how your donation to Tax Aid Africa may qualify for tax benefits.

About Tax Deductible Donations

Tax Aid and Enlightenment Initiative (Tax Aid Africa) is a registered not-for-profit organization dedicated to promoting voluntary tax compliance and supporting financially vulnerable individuals and small businesses across Nigeria.

Donations made to registered charitable organizations like ours may qualify for tax deductions under Nigerian tax law. This means you can potentially reduce your taxable income by the amount you donate, subject to applicable limits and regulations.

How Tax Deductible Donations Work

1

Make Your Donation

Donate to Tax Aid Africa through our secure online portal or bank transfer. All donations are recorded and acknowledged.

2

Receive Your Receipt

We will provide you with an official donation receipt that includes all details required for tax purposes.

3

Claim Your Deduction

When filing your tax returns, include your donation as a deductible expense. Consult with your tax advisor for specific guidance.

Potential Tax Benefits

For Individuals

Individual taxpayers may be eligible to deduct charitable donations from their assessable income, subject to limits specified by the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS).

For Businesses

Corporate entities may claim tax relief on donations made to approved charitable organizations. The deduction is typically limited to a percentage of total profits.

Important Notice

Tax laws and regulations can change. We strongly recommend consulting with a qualified tax professional or the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS) to understand how charitable donations may affect your specific tax situation. Tax Aid Africa does not provide tax advice.

What We Provide

To support your tax deduction claim, Tax Aid Africa provides the following documentation:

  • Official Donation Receipt - A formal acknowledgment of your contribution with date, amount, and our registration details.
  • Organization Registration Details - Our CAC registration number and relevant nonprofit registration documentation.
  • Annual Donation Summary - For regular donors, we provide an annual summary of all contributions made during the tax year.

Questions About Tax Deductions?

Our team is happy to provide the documentation you need. Contact us for more information.
